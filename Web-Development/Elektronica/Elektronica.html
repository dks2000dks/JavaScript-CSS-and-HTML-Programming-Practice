Welcome to Elektronica,

Electronics club of IIT Hyderabad

The Electronics Club aims at teaching and helping people understand the seemingly incomprehensible electronic gadgets in the world today, and also assists people in developing their own devices. It strives to create interest in the students for electronics. ELECTRONIKA was launched by a few students of IITH last year, with a vision to build a club that strives to create interest in electronics to all students.

Constitution

As of now, we have 2 co-ordinator and core members. Membership is given on the basis of enthusiasm shown in the various activities of the club throughout the semester.

Activities

Lectures

   The club organises several lectures covering various topics such as Basic Electronic,Micro-Controller programming and many others.


Hands-on Sessions

These are organised to give the members a flavor of circuits and other electrical components

The following sessions were organised till now: 1.Astable Multi-Vibrator Circuits.
2.Atmega16 Coding & simulation using Proteus.
3.Digital Clock which counts upto ten minutes with a precision of 100th of a second.



Quizzes & Competitions

Club conducts events such as Circuit Design Challenge and Electronic Bloopers(about debugging cicuits) during technical fests.



Current Projects

1.Voice Recgonition System.
2.Application of DTMF Decoder in controlling power appliances.
3.Working with ARM-Learning LPC218
