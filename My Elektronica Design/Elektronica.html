Elektronica Electronics Club

Welcome to Elektronica

The Electronics Club aims at teaching and helping students understand the seemingly incomprehensible electronic gadgets in the world today, and also assists people in developing their own devices. It strives to create interest in the students for electronics. ELEKTRONICA was launched by a few students of IITH the year 2012, with a vision to build a club that strives to create interest in electronics to all students and to help them excell in their projects.

As of now, we have 2 Co-Ordinators and 26 Core Members. Membership is given on the basis of enthusiasm shown in the various activities of the club throughout the semester.

Activities

Lectures

The club organises several lectures covering various topics such as Basic Electronics,Micro-Controller Programming,Basics of IOT, Basics of Machine Learning and many others topics.


Hands-On Sessions

These are organised by the members of club to a flavor of circuits and other electrical components to the students.

The following sessions were organised till now:

1. Introduction to Arduino and Basic Electric Components.
2. Interfacing Sensors and Bluetooth module with Arduino.
3. Astable Multi-Vibrator Circuits.
4. Atmega16 Coding & simulation using Proteus.
5. Digital Clock which counts upto ten minutes with a precision of 100th of a second.

Projects

  • Application of DTMF Decoder in controlling power appliances.

  • Working with ARM-Learning LPC218.

  • Image Classification.

  • Facial Recognition.

  • Vehicle Plate Recognition and connecting it with Government database.

Quizzes & Competitions


Club conducts events such as Circuit Design Challenge and Electronic Bloopers(about debugging circuits) during technical fests.
